About me

Unbeknownst to me at the time, I started my journey into software engineering in 2012. What started out as curious hobbying soon became a full-blown passion. I’ve had content management roles for various websites across multiple platforms – Joomla, Weebly, Sitecore, SharePoint – and began learning the basics of coding as a result. I couldn’t get enough.

September 2016, I had left my job and finished the first six weeks of pre-work for an 18-week coding bootcamp and, before the in-class portion started, we were asked to take the StrengthsFinder Assessment. After taking it, I found that ‘Learner’ is one of my top five strengths and I laughed out loud at this tidbit from one of the results guides:

‘Seek roles that require some form of technical competence. You will enjoy the process of acquiring and maintaining this expertise. As far as possible, shift your career toward a field with constantly changing technologies or regulations. You will be energized by the challenge of keeping up.’

It was like a strange little fortune cookie, portending my future that had already begun to unfurl. I smiled and nodded, feeling energized by what the future holds. Incidentally, my number one strength is 'Futuristic'.

Onward and upward!
